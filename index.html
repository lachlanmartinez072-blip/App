Getting started with investing

What's an ETF?

A single fund that holds hundreds of companies at once — buying one share buys you a slice of all of them, which is why it's already diversified.

Dollar-cost averaging

Investing a fixed amount on a regular schedule, regardless of price — smooths out the "did I buy at a bad time" worry.

Time in the market > timing it

Trying to predict dips tends to underperform just staying invested consistently, especially over 5+ year horizons.

Where to actually buy

Australian brokers with low/no fees on recurring buys include Pearler, CommSec Pocket, Stake, and Selfwealth — worth comparing before picking one.
